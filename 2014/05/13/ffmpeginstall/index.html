    What is ffmpeg ?

    FFmpeg is a complete, across-platform solution to record, convert and stream audio and video.

    Why use ffmpeg?

    • Play h.264 video
    • encoding capture video to h.264 video(It need ffmpeg h.264 module.)
    • publish a av stream
    • subscribe a rtsp av stream
