Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

English · 中文
Digital Breath does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information whatsoever. Digital Breath does not require an account, sign-in, or any form of registration.

2. Data Storage

All data generated by using the app — including game progress, plant growth status, energy, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using iOS's local storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

Digital Breath does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social SDKs. We do not use:

  • Firebase / Google Analytics
  • Facebook SDK
  • Crashlytics or Sentry
  • Any advertising network

4. Advertising

Digital Breath contains no advertisements of any kind. We do not display, serve, or track ads.

5. In-App Purchases

Digital Breath does not currently include in-app purchases. If this changes in a future version, this policy will be updated and the App Store listing will reflect it.

6. Children's Privacy

Digital Breath is appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or any age group, for that matter).

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how data is handled, we will update this page and the app's App Store listing. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

8. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us at:
support@digitalbreath.app
